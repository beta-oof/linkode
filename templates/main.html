About

Note: Misuse of this free service could result in a temporary or perm ban.

Linkode is a API service that allows you to protect your APIs from people trying to steal them and use them for malicious reasons. Linkode can also protect pictures, check the usage tab below for instructions on how to use linkode.

Usages (How To Use)

Protect API/Link: 0.0.0.0:8080/{url goes here}
Protect Images: 0.0.0.0:8080/{img url goes here}
